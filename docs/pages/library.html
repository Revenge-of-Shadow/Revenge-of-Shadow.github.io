It was a blissful transcendence to escape to whilst writing, but now I do not know anymore whether it was me who composed it all.

This book contains suicide attempts, detailed descriptions of melancholy, cold, affection, worry, fear, pain and music. This story turned out to be much more stressful than intended and thus I, for the first time, give a word of warning. This story might be mildly disturbing.
