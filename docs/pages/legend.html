Electroheart

The world

Not much is known.


The world is divided between two factions.


Technologists

Humans, industrialists.

They populate enormous cities with regulated biospheres.

Cities are connected by means of transportation across shrinking natural territories.

Outskirts of cities are off-limits, not by law but by interest. Those are wasted lands or construction sites of collosal builder robots.


Arcanists(homos aeternam)

Humans and hybrids of humans with highraces.

Conservative societies who live in natural territories in villages and towns.

They are in harmony with Aether; they study its sources and learn to interact with it depending on their individual talents and affinities.

People of high races are common in shared towns.


Technologists and arcanists are at the state of cold war for centuries.

The former spread the territories farther across the globe, but suffer from cataclysms or raids of well-guarded regions.

Technologists


It is told in legends of homos aeternam that there are Deeper Lands, but no one said where they are and what they store.


High races

Rightful elderly inhabitants of the world.

They live far away from the cities of technologists, in cities often hidden in landscapes or with the help of large Aether anomalies.


Technologists have accustomed themself to a reduced set of merged languages, whereas arcanists speak dialects of their regions, united with the knowledge of Latin.

High races rely on the languages of their individual cultures; however Latin is not uncommon for them.

People of shared towns might be familliar with both cultural sides.



The world itself has many places uncharted, given that the story is told from the perspective of William in the first book.


Wilderness

Unpopulated places, sometimes merging with the outskirts of arcanist towns.
