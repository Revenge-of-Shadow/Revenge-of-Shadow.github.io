He was talking to the whole class group, but I took it to my mind and remembered it very well.

This explains a lot, in fact: I study most of my time in university and at home because I find it hard to keep up the pace otherwise.

I aspire to learn every subject to its best.


In my free time I either create something of suffer from mental illnesses.


Very tricky memory: can forget anything, including the start of the sentence I was speaking; otherwise can remember some minor occurrence or phrase from a decade ago.

Identity

"I am more than one but less than two."

Earliest depiction of plurality.

I can not describe myself as just one person.

My thought patterns, reactions, interests and behaviour depend heavily on the current mental state.



I tried to describe it as a quadruple personality years ago.


William is my precise side. Self-control, cold mind, analysis, rationality. With that as well comes complete dissociation from any community and self-confidence mixed with additional considerations of mistakes.

Electroheart is the energy. Unrestricted power in creativity or just a daily haphazard. Might carry all kinds of tools and inventions along, get crazy, reckless and even suicidally dangerous ideas. Would willingly go for each of his wacky ideas and often succeed with disappointment because his plans never go as good as he imagined.

Quiffy is the lightness, the hand with the pencil of most gentle drawings; happy-go-lucky inline skater and nature lover.

Haywire is every mental disaster. Fear, panic, despair; also anguish and rage. This entity is the destruction. If anyone becomes a threat to me, I become closer to this.


However, I noticed that there are intermediate states I could not describe clearly, so I use the idea of four only approximately.



While we are unsure of how many we represent, we strive to sharpen.

I seek ways to set specific personality at the time of need.



I indulge in strong emotions, furthermore destabilizing myself to the state of resonance.

This is how I come to my genius works or art.

I might be not quite sane of mind.
