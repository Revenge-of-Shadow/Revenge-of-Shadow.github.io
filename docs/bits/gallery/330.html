Two years ago, on this cycle, I decided to assign female tone to the voice that judged me in my mind, calling her Jan without any second thoughts. This provided me with the most wholesome illusions for a year.
Then I was proven to be careless enough to descend from the grace and care about her much less than I should have had.
Now, however, I have my courage and care.

So this is Jeannette, character representation of Jan.
