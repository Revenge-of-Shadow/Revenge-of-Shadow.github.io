I went to university to conquer the world, but I can not keep up with the studies. I do as much as I can, but I can not keep up.
I do not understand bits from the recent lectures. And it was not too bad on mechanics tonight. But on mathematics I found myself lost in fear. I wished to simply collapse because I could not endure it. But I sat there and still felt the shock. I found myself unable to take notes. I was crying, just like I was crying before the lectures. I grabbed my sketchbook then and started drawing.
Surprisingly enough, it went fairly good and I finished it fast. It brought a slight relief, but I had another slight panic attack, after the lectures. I only made it out after a long conversation with Jan... in my head.

This Earth cycle I cried more than I did for the last several years. I still have such "spasms" of despair.
