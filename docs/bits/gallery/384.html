I dropped by at the youth club, saw her there. I thought she looks cute, so I sketched here in mere minutes. I wrote, "You are cute." on the other side of the sheet and gave it to her.
At first, she asked whether it was her. Then, she stood in shock for many seconds. Then, she asked to hug me and hugged sheepishly.

This is what I wanted: to be able to depict someone properly and with a touch of magic.
I wanted this for so long.
