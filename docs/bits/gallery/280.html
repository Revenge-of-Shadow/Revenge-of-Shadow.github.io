Autumn in the void



20241119 1617


I started drawing it on my first visit to the university. Since then I tried many approaches before I made it look good. I finished it the previous cycle. I spent the break between the lectures almost whole to finalize it. I was tired, my body hurt. And I was dazzled with how it felt to never rest.

The title of the picture has no hidden meaning. This is fall as it is in my imaginary underworld. If I envision book stories revolving in the main, defined world, then the underworld is left purely to my imagination. I can walk the futuristic city, lay on the hill during the eternal starry night. This is the place of freedom and no laws altogether.
